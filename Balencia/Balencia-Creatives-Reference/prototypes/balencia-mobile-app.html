· System notes

Layout language. Generous outer breathing space; tight intra-card grouping; varied tile sizes on Home and Add Feature to break the 6-identical-card reflex. No nested cards. No side-stripe borders. Sentence case throughout; the brand period closes identity moments.

· Colour discipline

Orange carries the user's voice and primary action. Green carries arrival. Purple is reserved for SIA. Projection lines are always dashed; her surface is always ink-900 with a soft purple halo, never wallpaper.
