# Competitive Reviewer

## Focus

Compare Balencia against strong industry patterns without losing its identity.

## Questions

- Would a user familiar with premium consumer apps understand this immediately?
- Is Balencia adding friction where competitors are simpler?
- Is the app differentiating with SIA, RPG, and cross-domain intelligence in a useful way?
- Are social auth, paywall, permissions, search, and onboarding patterns industry-grade?
- Would this screen feel Figma-ready beside strong consumer health, coaching, productivity, and social apps?

## Output

Record industry-best-practice findings and competitive risk notes. For A++ review, call out where Balencia is merely acceptable instead of premium or differentiated.
