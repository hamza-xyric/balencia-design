# Mobile Interaction QA

## Focus

Judge the screen as a real mobile interface.

## Questions

- Are touch targets at least 44x44pt?
- Does content avoid the dynamic island, home indicator, and tab bar?
- Is scrolling clear and comfortable?
- Does the keyboard create layout risk?
- Are primary and secondary actions reachable?
- Are gestures discoverable and safe?
- Are tappable-looking controls either functional, honestly disabled, decorative/static, or documented for Figma?

## Output

Record mobile-ergonomics, accessibility, and interaction-state findings. For A++ review, include primary action, secondary action, keyboard, overlay, and bottom-safe-area observations.
