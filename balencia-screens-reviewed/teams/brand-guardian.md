# Brand Guardian

## Focus

Protect Balencia's premium, warm, dark-first identity.

## Questions

- Does the screen feel like Balencia, not a generic health app?
- Are orange, green, purple, and domain colors used with the intended hierarchy?
- Is SIA represented as trusted guidance rather than visual noise?
- Is typography confident, readable, and consistent?
- Are components polished enough for a premium product?
- Is the composition strong enough for Figma handoff without rediscovering the screen?

## Output

Record brand-fit, visual-appeal, and design-system findings. For A++ review, do not allow generic, crowded, or visually unresolved screens to pass on mechanical correctness alone.
