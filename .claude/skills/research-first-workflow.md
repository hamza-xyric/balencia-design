# CORE-02: Research-First Workflow

**Version**: 2.1

---

## Purpose

Research industry best practices, competitive landscape, and trends **before** strategic tasks. Ensure recommendations are grounded in real data, not assumptions.

---

## When to Activate

**Automatic Triggers**:
- Business plans or strategies
- Technical architecture decisions
- Market analysis
- Pricing strategies
- Product feature planning

---

## Research Workflow

### Phase 1: Query
Formulate targeted search queries for the topic.

### Phase 2: Execute
Use WebSearch. Prioritize recent sources (< 12 months).

### Phase 3: Synthesize
Compile into structured findings with citations.

### Phase 4: Apply
Connect findings to the project's specific context.

---

## Citation Standards

### Format
```markdown
"Finding statement"
- Source: [Name]
- Date: [Month Year]
- Confidence: [High/Medium/Low]
```

### Source Quality

| Level | Source Type | Trust |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Primary | Official reports, academic papers | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Secondary | Industry publications, tech blogs | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Tertiary | News articles, company announcements | ⭐⭐⭐ |

---

## Research Quality Rules

### Must Include
- ✅ 3+ independent sources per major claim
- ✅ Publication dates (prefer < 12 months)
- ✅ Source credibility assessment
- ✅ Explicit limitations

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Single-source conclusions
- ❌ Outdated data without noting
- ❌ Unverifiable claims
- ❌ Correlation as causation

## Anti-Patterns

- Skipping research for "common knowledge"
- Using outdated data without noting age
- Presenting single sources as definitive
- Ignoring contradictory findings

---

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Strategic recommendations include citations
- [ ] Multiple sources corroborate claims
- [ ] Research is recent for fast-moving topics
- [ ] Limitations clearly stated
- [ ] Findings applied to project context
