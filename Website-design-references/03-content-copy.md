# 03 — Content & Copy (verbatim)

Every user-facing string from the experience, grouped for easy reuse. All copy is in SIA's first-person voice unless noted. These are exact transcriptions from the source (`scroll-scenes.ts`).

Each scene has a **title** (short heading) and a **body** (one line of SIA narration).

---

## Overview / framing copy

**Opening (Arrival)**
- Title: **The civilization revealed**
- Body: *A living intelligence powering every dimension of your life.*

**Climax (Cross-pillar revelation)** — Title: **Cross-pillar revelation**
This one advances through a **4-line sequence** as the viewer progresses (the payoff of the whole experience):
1. *This is the complete picture.*
2. *Most apps see one building. I see the entire civilization.*
3. *Your sleep affects your career. Your meals shape your mood. Your relationships drive your recovery.*
4. *Every dimension of human life becomes stronger when powered by intelligent AI.*

**Closing**
- Title: **The closing**
- Body: *Welcome to your civilization. Let us begin.*
- Brand tagline (closing frame): **Find your balance.**
- Wordmark: **Balencia.**
