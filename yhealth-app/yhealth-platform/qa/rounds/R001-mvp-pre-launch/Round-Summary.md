## Focus Areas

This round covers the full MVP. Pay special attention to:

1. **Onboarding completeness** — Can a brand-new user get through setup without confusion?
2. **Core health tracking** — Do the health pillars, scoring, and tracking feel valuable?
3. **AI Coach quality** — Are AI responses helpful, accurate, and personalized?
4. **Data presentation** — Do dashboards, charts, and scores make sense at a glance?
5. **Content quality** — Is all copy clear, professional, and free of errors?
