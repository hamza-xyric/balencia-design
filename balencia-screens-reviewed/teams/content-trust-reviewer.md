# Content And Trust Reviewer

## Focus

Review copy, consent, privacy, and emotional safety.

## Questions

- Is the copy clear, direct, and warm?
- Does the screen explain why sensitive information is needed?
- Are consent and privacy moments placed at the right time?
- Does any copy overpromise or feel manipulative?
- Are errors, empty states, and permissions respectful?
- Would a user understand what data SIA, providers, health, photos, billing, reports, or social surfaces can read or change?

## Output

Record trust/privacy, accessibility, and content findings. For A++ review, any unclear sensitive-data claim or overpromise should cap the grade until resolved.
