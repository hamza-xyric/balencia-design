# yHealth Platform - User Personas

> **Source:** Product Requirements Document v4.1, updated for Vision v5.0

---

## Primary Personas (MVP Focus)

### P1: The Whole-Life Optimizer (40% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Age Range** | 28-45 |
| **Profile** | Recognizes that health, career, relationships, and personal growth are all connected. Interested in holistic self-improvement |
| **Behavior** | Tracks fitness and nutrition but struggles to connect how sleep affects productivity, spending reflects stress, and mood drives everything |
| **Pain Points** | Life data scattered across 5+ apps, can't see cross-domain patterns, wants one intelligent companion |
| **Flexibility Preference** | Mixed — deep for insights and analysis, light for daily logging |

**Success Story:**
> "My AI coach showed me that my Sunday low energy wasn't just affecting Monday workouts — it was tanking my Tuesday meetings and triggering Wednesday takeout spending. Now I have a Sunday wind-down routine that improved my entire week."

---

### P2: The Busy Professional (25% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Age Range** | 30-50 |
| **Profile** | High-stress career, wants to perform better at work while maintaining health and relationships |
| **Behavior** | Sporadic health efforts, knows lifestyle affects everything but can't find time to connect the dots |
| **Pain Points** | Time-constrained, work stress bleeding into health and relationships, needs efficient coaching |
| **Flexibility Preference** | Light mode primary — values efficiency, voice calls while commuting |

**Success Story:**
> "2-minute daily check-ins keep my health and finances on track even during crazy work weeks. My AI coach noticed my stress-spending pattern before I did and helped me replace it with a 5-minute walk."

---

### P3: The Data-Driven Achiever (20% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Age Range** | 25-40 |
| **Profile** | Self-improvement obsessed, enjoys tracking everything, wants deep cross-domain analytics |
| **Behavior** | Active in fitness, personal development, and productivity optimization. Wants to see every correlation |
| **Pain Points** | Data overload without synthesis, uses 8+ apps that don't talk to each other, wants the unified view |
| **Flexibility Preference** | Deep mode — loves detailed analytics, knowledge graphs, and prediction tracking |

**Success Story:**
> "I discovered my workout timing directly impacts my creativity and that my best writing days follow specific sleep and nutrition patterns. My coach optimized my entire life routine around these correlations."

---

## Secondary Personas

### P4: The Habit Formation Seeker (10% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Age Range** | 25-55 |
| **Profile** | Struggles with consistency across all life areas, wants sustainable routines |
| **Flexibility Preference** | Light mode with gentle progression to deeper engagement |
| **Key Need** | Non-judgmental accountability, celebrates small wins, buddy matching for motivation |

---

### P5: The Gentle Growth Builder (5% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Age Range** | 30-60 |
| **Profile** | Prefers gentle approach, values emotional support, may be recovering from burnout or life transition |
| **Flexibility Preference** | Always light mode, gradual confidence building |
| **Key Need** | Compassionate, never overwhelming guidance across life dimensions |

---

## Persona Reference Matrix

| Persona | % Users | Mode | Key Value Proposition |
|---------|---------|------|----------------------|
| P1: Whole-Life Optimizer | 40% | Mixed | Cross-domain life insights |
| P2: Busy Professional | 25% | Light | Time-efficient whole-life coaching |
| P3: Data-Driven Achiever | 20% | Deep | Deep analytics across all domains |
| P4: Habit Formation Seeker | 10% | Light→Deep | Gentle accountability and buddy system |
| P5: Gentle Growth Builder | 5% | Light | Compassionate life support |

## Using Personas in Stories

When writing user stories, reference personas as:
- "As a Whole-Life Optimizer (P1)..."
- "As a Busy Professional (P2)..."
- "As a Data-Driven Achiever (P3)..."
- "As a Habit Formation Seeker (P4)..."
- "As a Gentle Growth Builder (P5)..."
