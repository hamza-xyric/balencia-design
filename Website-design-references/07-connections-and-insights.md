# 07 — Connections & Cross-Domain Insights

This is the **central differentiator** of the product, and strong "how it works" content for a website. The premise: life domains are not independent — they constantly affect each other, and SIA's value is seeing those links that single-purpose apps miss.

## The pitch, in SIA's words

> *Most apps see one building. I see the entire civilization.*

> *Your sleep affects your career. Your meals shape your mood. Your relationships drive your recovery.*

> *Every dimension of human life becomes stronger when powered by intelligent AI.*

In the 3D experience this was visualized two ways: **orange "energy" pipelines** running from the SIA Tower out to every domain (the AI powering each one), and **golden lines** firing *between* domains at the climax (the AI noticing one domain influencing another).

## Concrete cross-domain insight examples

These are the specific example connections authored for the experience — each pairs two domains with a plausible, concrete insight. They're written as the kind of finding SIA would surface, and they make excellent illustrative copy for a "connected intelligence" section. (Verbatim from `energy-system/cross-connections/SPEC.md`.)

| # | Connection | Insight |
|---|------------|---------|
| 1 | **Fitness ↔ Recovery & Sleep** | "Recovery score impacts tomorrow's workout capacity" |
| 2 | **Nutrition ↔ Career** | "Skipped meals on meeting days reduce afternoon focus 31%" |
| 3 | **Relationships ↔ Wellbeing** | "Social connection improves recovery scores by 24%" |
| 4 | **Finance ↔ Career** | "Spending increases 40% during high-stress work weeks" |
| 5 | **Recovery & Sleep ↔ AI Analytics** | "Evening meditation correlates with next-day focus scores" |
| 6 | **Communication ↔ Relationships** | "You haven't spoken to [name] in 14 days" |

> The percentages (31%, 24%, 40%) are illustrative example figures from the prototype copy, not validated product metrics — treat them as placeholder demonstrations of the *kind* of insight, and confirm before publishing any as fact.

## How it surfaces in the product

The "Today screen" (see [`06-product-mockup.md`](06-product-mockup.md)) turns this concept into a single live feature: the **Intelligence path** (Recovery → Career → Nutrition) shows three domains chaining into one recommendation ("Sleep debt plus meeting load → Move deep work before 2 PM"). That's the cross-domain idea made tangible in one daily card.

## Per-domain "Signal" lines reinforce the same idea

Every domain's preview includes a **Signal** line that names *which other domains feed into it* — another ready-made way to express connectedness. For example:
- Fitness signal: *"Sleep quality and nutrition adjust effort targets."*
- Career signal: *"Sleep and nutrition adjust task timing."*
- Recovery signal: *"Training load and stress set recovery priority."*

The full set is in [`02-districts.md`](02-districts.md).
