# 04 — The Narrative Journey (17 beats)

The original experience was a single scroll of **17 scenes** lasting about **5 minutes** (300 seconds of timeline mapped across a tall scroll stage). This is the **messaging spine** — useful whether the new website is a scroll-story, a sequence of sections, or a multi-page layout. The 3D camera coordinates are intentionally omitted here (they live in source; see [`08-source-pointers.md`](08-source-pointers.md)).

## Shape of the arc

```
Arrival  →  SIA reveal (×2)  →  11 domain deep-dives  →  Cross-pillar climax  →  Product reality  →  Closing CTA
 (1)            (2–3)                 (4–14)                    (15)                  (16)              (17)
```

1. **Arrival** — pull back to reveal the whole city. Establish awe and scale. *"A living intelligence powering every dimension of your life."*
2. **SIA Tower reveal** — meet SIA. *"I am SIA. The intelligence core, emotional engine, and life operating system."*
3. **Inside SIA Tower** — go inside the "neural core" where signals are synthesized. *"The atrium turns every signal into one living plan."*
4. **Fitness** — first domain. *"Your body tells a story every day. I read every chapter."*
5. **Yoga & Wellbeing** — *"Calm is not the absence of energy. It is energy perfectly directed."*
6. **Finance** — *"I see the pattern before your bank does."*
7. **Knowledgebase** — *"I never forget what works for you."*
8. **Chat & Communication** — *"I listen to understand, not just respond."*
9. **Leaderboard & Competition** — *"Twenty one days is not a streak. It is a habit."*
10. **Relationships** — *"The people in your life shape your health more than any workout."*
11. **Career** — *"I see how sleep, stress, and relationships power your performance."*
12. **Recovery & Sleep** — *"Recovery is where today's patterns become tomorrow's plan."*
13. **AI Analytics** — *"This is where I think."*
14. **Nutrition** — *"What you eat connects to how you sleep, think, and perform."*
15. **Cross-pillar revelation (the climax)** — pull back again; everything lights up and connections fire between domains. The 4-line payoff sequence runs here (see [`03-content-copy.md`](03-content-copy.md)), ending on the central message.
16. **The Today screen (product reality)** — the city resolves into the actual app UI: one clear daily insight (see [`06-product-mockup.md`](06-product-mockup.md)).
17. **The closing** — final wide frame + brand + CTA. *"Welcome to your civilization. Let us begin."*

## Structural notes for a website rebuild (descriptive)

- The arc is **three overview "breath" moments** (scenes 1, 15, 17) bracketing **two focused acts**: the SIA introduction (2–3) and the 11 domain tour (4–14).
- Domains were ordered: Fitness → Yoga → Finance → Knowledgebase → Chat → Leaderboard → Relationships → Career → Recovery → Analytics → Nutrition. This isn't alphabetical or by importance; it alternates `surge`/`calm`/`focus` moods to keep a rhythm (see tones in [`02-districts.md`](02-districts.md)).
- In the 3D version most domain scenes had an **exterior → interior** reveal (approach the building, then go inside). For a website this maps to a "card/summary → detail" pattern per domain.
- The climax (15) is where the **cross-domain connections** are the message — that content is in [`07-connections-and-insights.md`](07-connections-and-insights.md).
- Scene 16 deliberately drops from "epic city" to "a person holding a phone" — the grand metaphor cashing out into a concrete, calm daily plan.
